This is one of my favorite MTB routes in the area - a perfect mix of flow trails, technical sections, and stunning views. The climb is tough but manageable, and the descent is absolutely worth it.

## Highlights

- **Flow Trail Section**: About 3km of perfectly maintained single trail with berms and jumps
- **Technical Climb**: Rocky switchbacks that test your skills
- **Panorama Point**: 360Â° views of the Karwendel range at the highest point

## Trail Conditions

- Dry and dusty in exposed sections
- Some loose rocks on the steep parts
- A few muddy spots in the forest sections

## Bike Setup

- **Bike**: Canyon Spectral CF 8
- **Tires**: Schwalbe Magic Mary front, Hans Dampf rear
- **Suspension**: 150mm front, 140mm rear
- **Dropped**: 0 chains (finally!)

The bike handled everything perfectly. I've been really happy with the Spectral's versatility - climbs well enough but really shines on the descents.

Next time I want to try the alternative descent via the old logging road, which is supposed to be even faster.
